How to complete this assessment

This assessment helps your Scarlet Spark consulting team understand how your organization currently operates. There are no right or wrong answers — we're looking for an honest picture of where things stand today, not where you'd like them to be.

Scoring
For each item, select the score that best reflects your current reality:
0 — Not in place: This doesn't exist yet
1 — Needs improvement: Something exists but it's incomplete, inconsistent, or not working well
2 — Good enough for now: This is in place and working reasonably well for your current stage
3 — Great: This is solid and you'd not change it right now
Your responses
Share a brief description of what you currently have in place — or why something isn't in place yet. A few sentences is enough. We're most interested in what actually exists (not what's planned), any challenges or tensions you've noticed, and context that might help us understand your score.
Documentation
For each item, please share a link to the relevant document rather than just noting that one exists — we need to be able to read it to assess it properly. Upload to Google Drive or Dropbox, set sharing to "Anyone with the link can view", then paste the link in the documentation field.
🔗
Save & return
Your progress saves automatically as you fill in the form. A save link will appear at the top of the page — copy and bookmark it so you can return and continue where you left off at any time.
