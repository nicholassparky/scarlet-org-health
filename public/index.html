Your progress 0% How to complete this assessment This assessment helps your Scarlet Spark consulting team understand how your organization currently operates. There are no right or wrong answers — we're looking for an honest picture of where things stand today, not where you'd like them to be. SCORING For each item, select the score that best reflects your current reality: 0 — Not in place: This doesn't exist yet 1 — Needs improvement: Something exists but it's incomplete, inconsistent, or not working well 2 — Good enough for now: This is in place and working reasonably well for your current stage 3 — Great: This is solid and you'd not change it right now YOUR RESPONSES Share a brief description of what you currently have in place — or why something isn't in place yet. A few sentences is enough. We're most interested in what actually exists (not what's planned), any challenges or tensions you've noticed, and context that might help us understand your score. DOCUMENTATION For each item, please share a link to the relevant document rather than just noting that one exists — we need to be able to read it to assess it properly. Upload to Google Drive or Dropbox, set sharing to "Anyone with the link can view", then paste the link in the documentation field. 🔗 SAVE & RETURN Your progress saves automatically as you fill in the form.
